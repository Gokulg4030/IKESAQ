President's Message

Warm greetings and heartiest welcome! Back in 2019 - With the aim for improving the livelihood of expats in QATAR, A group of people sharing similar thoughts and ideas met, together - discussed and started an organisation - 'IKESAQ".

IKESA has been  a multidisciplinary platform for anyone who could exhibit their talent - let it be in ARTS , SPORTS , CULTURAL , SOCIO ECONOMIC or even as a volunteer who could deliver their service for the betterment of society 

We didnot limit ourself merely as an  organisation ,rather we expanded and diversified our public welfare activities by starting online job portal , medical wing , medical helpline , sports wing , ladies cultural wing and social media pages for public interaction

Recent covid 19 pandemic made the journey a kind of rollar coster ride for us , but still with the support of all the respected members and the administrative team , IKESAQ emerged as one of the most prominent and unavoidable  organisation in QATAR

Visiting our web portal will help you , discover more about us & our activities as torch bearers in bringing constructive changes to the society 

Looking forward to a successful and gratifying future 

"Lets be together"
