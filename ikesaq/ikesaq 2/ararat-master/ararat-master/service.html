About Our Services

To uphold the values of our country and its people and as a gratitude to Qatar for the opportunities and facilities provided, our association is part of many services on regular basis.

Few of such services we are part of are.

Medical Camps and Events : As many of our members are from medical field, our association frequently organizes medical events and camps in support of local health bodies and individually.

Consulting and Support : We provide Consultation and support to our fellow member and member of the community with advice and guidance in case of any support required.

Job Portal : We have a dedicated job wing, and we actively post Job details in the market through our dedicated WhatsApp groups for Jobs.

Community Outreach : We work closely with ICF and other organizations under Indian Embassy and are part of activities and events which need our presence.

Volunteer Opportunities : We offer a variety of volunteer opportunities for individuals and groups who want to make a difference in their community.
